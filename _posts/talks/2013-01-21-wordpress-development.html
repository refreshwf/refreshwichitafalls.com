As web developers we have a choice. We can choose to develop static pages, or we can choose to work with content management systems (CMS's). This month Eric Binnion will give us a look into WordPress. WordPress's power comes from the fact that it has a huge following. There are currently over 59,000,000 websites using WordPress worldwide and there are over 1,600 free theme in the WordPress repositories.

As developers, WordPress has many features that are attractive to us. Eric will cover the basics of WordPress theme and plugin development. You can expect to hear topics such as: ajax, working with settings API, how to use options, creating custom meta boxes, how to use wp-cron, and how to convert a static website into a WordPress theme.

Eric is a computer science student at MSU and Community Manager for Microbrand Media. Follow him on Twitter.

[This talk will be part 1 of a 3 part talk about blogging and content management systems. Part 1 focuses on WordPress, part 2 will focus on Drupal, and part 3 will focus on static generators like Jekyll.]
