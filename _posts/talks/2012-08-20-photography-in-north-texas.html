This month Jake and Jeanette VanDonge will be sharing their experiences as photographers in North Texas. Discussing how they got started, the equipment they use, showcasing some of their work and answering any questions. Photography is much more then a job or a hobby for these two.

Jake and Jeanette VanDonge are a photojournalistic husband & wife photography team based in North Texas. They are owners of VanD Photography, photojournalists for KFDX-TV3 and run Eat Local Texoma, a popular Facebook community showcasing great local restaurants in the Texoma area.
