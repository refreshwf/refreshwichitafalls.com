Build more with LESS CSS

Ever thought how much better CSS would be if you only had variables or expressions or "x" or "y". Tired of remembering all of the browser prefixes for the new CSS3 features? Enter LESS CSS. LESS CSS is a CSS pre-processor that offers variables, mixins, expressions and much more. This is how everyone dreamed CSS would be ran. In this session, you will learn how to fully harness all the functionality of LESS while avoiding some of the pitfalls many new users face. CSS3 will never seem easier!

Jake Smith is a software engineer for iSight Partners in Dallas, Texas and the co-organizer of the Dallas PHP group. He played football at and graduated from Midwestern State University. He has spoken at several conferences and uncons including Dallas PHP, Lone Star PHP, Confoo, PHP Tek, and HTML5.tx.

Preparing a Resume

Sally Henschel will discuss the characteristics of resumes that are important to potential employers and review the process of preparing and formatting job application materials: paper and electronic resumes, the job application letter, and follow-up correspondence after an interview.

Sally Henschel received her Ph.D. in Technical Communication and Rhetoric from Texas Tech University and her M.A. and B.A. in English from Midwestern State University, where she teaches in the Department of English.
