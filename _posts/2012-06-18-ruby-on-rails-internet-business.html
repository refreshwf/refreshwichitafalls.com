An introduction to building a Ruby on Rails application.

Come learn what all the craze is about as Adam Hernandez demonstrates the process of creating a Ruby on Rails application from the ground-up. He will discuss the basics to building an application using the model-view-controller paradigm and why it is important to maintain this core design pattern. He will show you how to quickly and easily install valuable time-saving 3rd-party plugins and gems that will assist you in crafting your dream Rails application. And lastly, he will discuss what an object-relational mapper is and how it can be used to retrieve data without having to write a single line of SQL.

Adam Hernandez graduated from Midwestern State University in 2006 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. He is lead software engineer for a shopping cart platform being developed at Digimedia.

Turning your hobby into a small business on the internet.

Mitzi and Charlie Lewis will share a story of how the internet and social media played a role in the transitioning of a hobby into a small business. They'll walk you through this progression and how they utilize forums, Facebook, Youtube, and a website to grow the business and brand.

Mitzi Lewis teaches at Midwestern State University and Charlie Lewis makes straight razors. See how they have merged an old-school trade with modern technologies to leverage the long tail of the internet and come up with a workable business.
