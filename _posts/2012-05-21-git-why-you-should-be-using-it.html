Jorin Slaybaugh gives you a brief introduction to Git and why it is important that you use version control on every project. Whether working with a team on enterprise software or just working solo on a small project, Git is a resource everyone should have in their tool belt. He will walk you through the basics of creating repos, making commits, and switching branches. He will also introduce Github's software/services and show why it is more than just one of Silicon Valley's fastest growing companies - it is an invaluable addition to every one of your repositories.

Jorin graduated from Midwestern State University and is the lead developer and VP of Technology for E-vent Software. He is a .NET guy with a love of jQuery and using MVC to make API's for single-page JavaScript web apps. You can find his blog at cacheandquery.com, follow him on twitter or browse his repositories on github.
