Refresh WF is proud to host the premiere of Simeon Hendrix' documentary film "Simeon Hendrix Goes to Cleveland to Document WMC Fest." This documentary follows Simeon as he journeys from dreaming to brainstorming to gathering sponsors to enjoying and interviewing some of the biggest artists in the world. WMC Fest is the premier art, design and music event in the Midwest with a unique focus on cutting edge graphic design. Simeon raised over $400 for the Boys & Girls Club of Wichita Falls during his campaign to secure funds for the documentary. Don't forget to check out the trailer on YouTube.

Simeon Hendrix is a professional graphic artist here in Wichita Falls, Texas. In addition to his love of visual art, he also toured the US for 9 years as the frontman for the experimental rock band DOWN-STARES.
